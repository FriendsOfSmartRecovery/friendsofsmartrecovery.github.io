Community is a key part of recovery. The Friends of SMART Recovery Discord server provides a space to stay connected between meetings.


What is Discord?


Discord is a free, 24/7 chat app that hosts many communities (called "servers"). In the Friends of SMART Recovery Discord server, peers support one another, talking about healthy coping mechanisms, reminding each other that they are not alone in their struggles.


The server is created, managed, and attended by SMART-trained faciliators, ensuring that the server is a safe environment to be in. The server also has channels specific for Friends and Family of people who struggle with addiction, as well as more lighthearted sections to pass the time.


Why Join?


  • 24/7 Support from people who understand your journey
  • Real-Time Discussions to stay engaged between meetings
  • Anonymity to protect your privacy
